What Is MyChart?

MyChart is a secure, online tool that gives you convenient, 24/7 access to your personal health information. Designed with patients in mind, MyChart connects directly with your healthcare provider’s electronic medical record system, allowing you to view and manage important aspects of your care.

No more waiting on hold, mailing paper forms, or wondering when your results will arrive. With MyChart, everything you need to stay informed is just a few clicks away.

Key Features of MyChart

  • Schedule Appointments

    • Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments with your primary care doctor or specialist directly through the portal.

  • View Test Results

    • See lab and imaging results as soon as they’re available—no more waiting for a call or letter.

  • Manage Medications

    • Request prescription refills and view current medications at any time.

  • Access Medical Records

    • View your medical history, immunization records, visit summaries, and more.

  • Message Your Care Team

    • Have a non-urgent question? Send a secure message to your provider and get a response without making a phone call.

  • View and Pay Bills

    • Check your balance, view statements, and make secure payments online.

  • Proxy Access

    • Manage care for a child, aging parent, or dependent by requesting proxy access to their account.

Safe and Secure

We understand how important privacy is when it comes to your personal health information. MyChart uses the latest encryption and security features to ensure that your data stays protected. Only you—and those you authorize—can access your medical records.

Accessible Anytime, Anywhere

You can use MyChart on your desktop, tablet, or through the MyChart mobile app, available for download on iOS and Android devices. Whether you're checking your appointment schedule or reviewing test results, everything you need is just a tap away.

Your Health, Your Way

At My Chart Guide, we believe your health information should be at your fingertips. The My Chart Guide Patient Portal gives you the tools to be an informed, active participant in your own care. From routine visits to complex care coordination, MyChart is here to simplify the way you manage your health.
