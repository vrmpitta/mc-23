About Our MyChart Patient Portal

we believe that great healthcare starts with strong communication and easy access to information. That’s why we offer MyChart, a secure and convenient patient portal designed to keep you connected to your health, your care team, and your medical records—anytime, anywhere. MyChart is more than just a digital tool—it's a reflection of our commitment to patient-centered care. Whether you're managing a chronic condition, scheduling your next visit, or simply keeping track of your wellness goals, MyChart helps you stay informed and in control.

Empowering Patients Through Access

Our goal is to empower you with the tools you need to make informed decisions about your health. With MyChart, you can take a more active role in your care without needing to make extra phone calls or visits. You’ll have secure access to your medical history, test results, medications, and appointment information—all from your computer or mobile device.


Features include:

  • Real-Time Access to Test Results - View lab and imaging results as soon as they’re released.

  • Appointment Scheduling and Management - Schedule or change upcoming appointments with ease.

  • Secure Messaging - Communicate with your care team directly through the portal.

  • Prescription Management - Request refills and see current medications in one place.

  • Bill Pay and Insurance Info - View statements, check your balance, and make payments online.

  • Proxy Access - Easily manage care for your children, elderly parents, or other dependents.

A Seamless Part of Our Care Experience

MyChart integrates directly with our electronic health record system, allowing your care team to stay up to date and giving you real-time insights into your health journey. From pre-visit check-ins to follow-up instructions, MyChart helps ensure that every step of your care experience is connected and efficient. We offer MyChart as part of our broader mission to improve access, reduce barriers, and provide a seamless, modern healthcare experience. It’s our way of keeping you at the center of everything we do.

Safe, Secure, and Always Available

Your privacy is our top priority. MyChart uses the latest in encryption and security protocols to ensure your health information remains confidential and accessible only to you and authorized individuals. You can access MyChart anytime, day or night, from any internet-connected device—including the MyChart mobile app for iOS and Android.
